A toast that isn't actually from France!

Ingredients:

  • 1 loaf of white bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 2/3 cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

Instructions:

  1. Cut the loaf of white bread into slices
  2. Mix eggs, milk, cinnamon, vanilla extract, salt together in a bowl
  3. Oil the pan with butter
  4. Soak bread slices in the mixture and fry on the pan until golden brown and serve
