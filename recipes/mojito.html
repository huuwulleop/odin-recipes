@therealmojito wants to follow you

Ingredients:

  • 10 mint leaves
  • 1/2 lime cut into 3 wedges
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 1 cup of ice cubes
  • 1 1/2 ounces of rum
  • 1/2 cup of club soda

Instructions:

  1. Crush mint and lime together until oil and juice
  2. Crush lime and sugar
  3. Pour rum and club soda into a cup of ice cubes
  4. Mix with the juice and serve
